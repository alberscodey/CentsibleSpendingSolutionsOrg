Rebecca's Story

Our founder and president, Rebecca Rupp, has a passion for budget counseling, and believes anyone can achieve their financial goals. Sometimes we just need a little help along the way. And she understands the struggle: Rebecca grew up in a house where every penny counted. As she grew up, Rebecca knew that she loved numbers and wanted to make them work out better not only for her own finances, but for others' as well. She didn't want to achieve financial freedom for just herself; she wanted to pass that freedom on to others, too. Rebecca began taking courses in 1996 to learn different budgeting strategies and wealth building techniques. In 1998, she began sharing what she had learned and experienced. In 2007, Rebecca started her own business counseling folks in financial need. In 2015, she developed the nonprofit, Centsible Spending Solutions (that's us!) to expand her reach for helping others live their dreams. She's a certified Crown Financial Budget Coach with over 20 years of experience under her belt. Journey along with us!
